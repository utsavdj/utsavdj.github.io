My Research.

Influence and Effectiveness of Advertising on Consumer Buying Behavior in Nepal (2015) – Final Year Research project

  • Collected data from 100 respondents at various motorbike showrooms in Kathmandu, Nepal by distributing questionnaires
  • Performed quantitative analysis of the collected data through exploratory factor analysis and multiple regression with the help of data analysis and statistical software called SPSS
  • Concluded that advertising was not very effective in influencing consumer buying behavior in Nepal since, in the absence of advertisements, the environment surrounding the consumers equally influenced their buying behavior
  • Recommended ways by which this research can be used by advertisers, advertisement agencies and media to make advertising more effective in influencing consumer buying behavior in Nepal
